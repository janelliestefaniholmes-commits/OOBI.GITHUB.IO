# OOBI – Food Matrix Innovation Lab

Research and innovation in food matrices and functional foods.  
OOBI integrates bioinformatics simulation, laboratory validation and regulatory consulting to develop scientifically supported food products.

## About Us
OOBI explores novel Peruvian plant species and underutilized organic residues as sources of bioactive compounds. Our work combines computational modeling, experimental validation and food safety strategy to transform natural resources into functional food solutions.

## Research Areas
- Exploration of novel Peruvian plant matrices
- Functional food design and bioactive compound research
- Antioxidant systems in plant-based matrices
- Fermentation and probiotic formulations
- Valorization of organic residues and by-products
- Food safety and regulatory compliance

## Plant Matrices Under Study
Current research focuses on bioactive potential from the following species:

- Cacao – *Theobroma cacao*  
- Coffee – *Coffea arabica*  
- Sachajergón – *Dracontium loretense*  
- Black grape – *Vitis vinifera*  
- Manayupa – *Desmodium molliculum*  
- Cañihua – *Chenopodium pallidicaule*  
- Pitahaya – *Selenicereus undatus*

## Scientific Approach
Our research workflow integrates:

1. **Bioinformatics modeling** to predict bioactive interactions  
2. **Laboratory validation** using biochemical and spectrophotometric assays  
3. **Food matrix formulation** and optimization  
4. **Functional product development**  
5. **Food safety compliance** aligned with regulatory frameworks

## Organic Residue Valorization
OOBI also investigates the transformation of agricultural and food processing residues into valuable functional ingredients, promoting sustainability and circular bioeconomy approaches.

## Regulatory and Food Safety Consulting
Support for food companies in regulatory compliance and quality management:

- Sanitary registration
- Sanitary authorization
- PGH certification
- HACCP validation
- Documentation management aligned with DIGESA requirements

## Applications
- Research project formulation/development
- Development of functional beverages
- Antioxidant-rich food matrices
- Bioactive ingredient formulation
- Scientific support for food innovation projects
